START EMPOWERING BALI

ALL-in Cultural Immersion Research Program is a 6 days program where students collaborate with a local village in Bali to conduct field research on their agricultural practices. Through data analysis and strategic recommendations, students will play a vital role in driving direct impact to improve farming practices and enhance the local economy.

In addition to research, this program offers personal development sessions on leadership and teamwork through culture immersion, empowering students to be catalysts for positive change and fostering social responsibility and empathy.
