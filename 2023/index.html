ALL-in Community Empowerment Program
is a 8-day summer program that combines live-in experience, personal project design and implementation, and self-development sessions.

The program will be held in the rural areas of Salatiga, Central Java, where students are able to immerse themselves in the local community, identify the challenges and opportunities among the people, and make a direct impact through their own personal projects.

Community Immersion

What you will do:
  • Interact within a community
  • Experience the local culture, values, and social interaction
  • Actively participate in the personal development of the locals
Skills you will acquire:
Empathy and Compassion
Creative Thinking
Problem Solving
Adaptability and Life Skills
Communication
Your social impact:

Bring creative solutions to solve real-world problems while also Implementing knowledge, values, and innovation in real life


Character Development Activities

What you will do:
  • Engaging and fun outdoor games
  • Self-development sessions
  • Design thinking framework to craft your take-home project
Skills you will acquire:
Teamwork and Personal Proactiveness
Creative Thinking
Problem Solving
Project Management
Your social impact:

Produce a relevant, ready-to-implement project plan


Community Empowerment Projects

What you will do:
  • Inspire and motivate underprivileged youth through fun activities
  • Compose fun workshops in digital literacy, financial literacy, and visual design
Skills you will acquire:
Self-awareness and Tolerance
Empathy and Compassion
Creative Thinking
Teamwork and Proactiveness
Public Speaking
Your social impact:

Equipping the underprivileged community with specific practical skills for to become a digital native generation
